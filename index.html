Welcome to PC Club

About Us

We are a computer science club located at River City High School in West Sacramento California. Established in 2024, we are a group of students who love coding and creating new and exciting programs. Our mission is to have a place where students can expand and explore all the possibilities that Computer Science can offer them, in order for them to be able to go beyond all that they can be. PC Club life is great! We have meetings once a week for the convenience of the students, and the schedule is adjustable if needed, because PC Club is for the students by the students. At PC Club we have a great team, who are always willing to help club members if they every run into any difficulties. But PC Club is not just all work, it's a whole lot of fun too; with occasional Uno battles and lots of drone flying and Lego building! Hey everyone, this is our second post on our blog and in this post we are going to talk about the history of the PC Club. Established in 2024 at River City High School in West Sacramento, California, a group of computer science loving students noticed that their high school had almost everything except for a computer science club. Determined to create a safe place where any software intrigued student can rightfully explore their minds, this group of students worked together with their amazing Computer science teacher, Ms. Warrior, to create the school's first Computer Science club. And later it would be called PC club as an acronym to the phrase : People Coding.

Hey everyone, this is our Fourth post on our blog and in this post we are going to talk about the benefits of being in the PC Club. One of the most significant benefits of being in the PC club is that you have a place to creatively explore your mind and test out any programs or software projects you might not have been able test out in class. Another benefit of joining PC Club is that you get to participate in workshops with graduate students from UC Davis, and meet with professionals working in the Computer Science and Data Science fields. And of course and important benefit to every high school student is that this club looks great on one's resume!

ðŸŒŸ Our Fourth Post

Hey everyone, this is our third post on our blog and in this post we are going to talk about what a typical day at the PC club looks like. The first thing that we do when we have a club meeting is that we go through our daily announcements, which usually consist of upcoming events or project due dates. Then we work on our projects, talking among our teammates and utilizing the white board when needed. After working on a project we usually test our components out, whether that be running a website or flying a drone. Then when everyone is done working for the day, we pack up, make sure we all know the plan for the rest of the week, sign our names on the attendance sheet, and say our goodbyes (At least until next week!).
